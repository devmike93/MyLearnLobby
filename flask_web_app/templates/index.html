MyLearnLobby

A centralized e-learning tool helps manage self-learning and courses from various sources.

GET STARTED

Comprehensive Overview of All Planned Educational Activities

This feature provides users with a detailed overview of all their planned educational activities, allowing them to easily track their progress across multiple courses and learning resources. It helps in organizing study schedules, keeping deadlines in check, and ensuring that no important task is overlooked.

Advanced Time Management Tools to Keep Track of Progress and Deadlines

Our tool includes a suite of time management features designed to help users manage their study time efficiently. With built-in calendars, reminders, and progress tracking, users can stay on top of their learning goals and ensure they are meeting all their deadlines without stress.

Seamless Integration with Various Learning Resources for Unified Management

This feature enables seamless integration with a wide range of learning resources, allowing users to manage all their educational content from a single platform. Whether it's online courses, e-books, or research papers, users can access and organize their learning materials effortlessly.

About Our Journey and Inspiration

Our platform was created by a dedicated team of professionals with a passion for delivering quality service.

This project was inspired by the need to provide learners with a comprehensive tool to manage their educational activities from multiple sources. The journey started in Augest 2023 and has been an enriching experience.
This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
